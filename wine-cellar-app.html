To sync across all your devices, enter your Firebase project credentials below. This only needs to be done once per device.

How to get these values:
1. Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with your Google account
2. Open your project → click the ⚙ gear → Project settings
3. Scroll down to Your apps → click your web app (or create one)
4. Copy the values from the firebaseConfig object shown there
